Super Visa

What is the Super Visa? The Super Visa is an option for parents and grandparents of Canadian citizens and permanent residents to visit their family in Canada. These individuals may be eligible to apply for the Parent and Grandparent Super Visa to visit their family in Canada for up to 2 years without the need to renew their status. What are the requirements for a Super Visa application? To obtain a Parent or Grandparent Super Visa for Canada, applicants must have valid insurance. Super Visa applications need to provide proof that they have private medical insurance from a Canadian insurance company valid for a minimum of 1 year from a Canadian insurance company and that it: Covers healthcare, hospitalization and repatriation Provides a minimum coverage of $100,000 Is valid for each entry to Canada and available for review by a port of entry officer For more details, visit the Government of Canada's Parent and Grandparent Super Visa website.

PARMJIT'S Journey

Paramjit Gill, born and raised in the village of Khokhar, Punjab came to Canada in 1989. Like most immigrants, he had to start from the bottom and overcome his share of barriers. In 1991, he moved to Quesnel, B.C. where he became a tree planter. Paramjit, a believer in securing his future decided to get his First Aid level three certification and his Gradermen ticket. With his qualifications, eventually Paramjit was able to fill a position in the local mill, where he worked for seventeen years and raised his family. In 2007, he wanted to take the next step in his career, as felt he had done a great job raising his family and wanted to become a business man. He passed is L.L.Q.P and became an Insurance Agent. Like any business he knew he would have struggles along the way. With determination, hard work and the continuous support of his family, Paramjit is now a leading insurance adviser in B.C. offering his services to cities all across the beautiful province of British Columbia.

Paramjit is a caring individual, who strives to meet all the needs of his clients, no matter the challenge.
